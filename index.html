Airlines Celestial

Having carved a remarkable name for itself in the world of restaurants serving the most delicious meals since its opening, Airlines Celestial Restaurant has been one of the most memorable venues for families to gather and bond for over 23 years. Situated on Kozhikode Road, Malappuram, Kerala, this restaurant was founded in 1996. Catering to over 1000 customers a day, our committed and cheerful staff are always ready to serve you some of the most nostalgic recipes that people keep coming back for. ‘Healthy eating’ is never compromised upon by our skilled chefs and our patrons are served with love, patience and passion. We are equipped with an in-house prayer hall as well as a kids play area to serve our guests better.
